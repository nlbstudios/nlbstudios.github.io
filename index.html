Opening a New Chapter

Bye-Buy Dental Practice not only provides a roadmap to guide dentists through the dental practice sales process, but also contains the tools, documents and support necessary for you to be able to sell your dental practice without a broker. Bye-Buy Dental Practice gives step-by-step instructions beginning with preparing your practice for sale, marketing your practice to find the right buyer, qualifying buyers effectively to save time (and headaches), negotiating the sale, and facilitating a smooth transition to the new owner – all without giving up any of the profits.

What is the “Bye-Buy Dental Practice” program?

Bye-Buy Dental Practice is a turn-key solution for dentists who want to sell their dental practice without paying brokerage fees.

Sell Your Practice Now - Without a Broker

Selling a dental practice involves much more than just finding a buyer. Understanding how to prepare your practice to attract the right buyers, qualifying buyers so you don’t waste your time on those that are not real buyers. Pricing your practice competitively by understanding your market, and negotiating the details of the sale itself are all complex components where critical errors can be made. Bye-Buy Dental Practice has processes in place to ensure that you avoid the pitfalls and mistakes that are common in dental practice sales.

Bye-Buy Dental Practice gives you step-by- step guidance, as well as all the necessary tools and documents that will enable you to:
  • Establish your practice value
  • Prepare yourself and your practice for the sale
  • Construct an advertisement to attract potential buyers
  • Locate and qualify buyers
  • Negotiate the sale and post-sale agreement
  • Inform the staff and patients and work with them to complete a smooth, successful transition
The book also contains:
  • Worksheets to Assess Practice Value
  • Advertising Guidelines and Sample Ad
  • Prospective Buyer Confidentiality Agreement
  • Tips on Showing the Practice and Follow-up
  • Purchase Offer Template and Sample
  • Term Sheet Template and Sample
  • Practice Sale Agreement Template and Sample
  • Buyer Closing Checklist
  • Seller Closing Checklist
  • Sammple Letter to Patients

Bye-Buy Dental Practice Includes:

The instructional book "Bye-Buy Dental Practice - How to Sell Your Dental Practice Without a Broker"

This book contains all of the information, check-lists, worksheets, templates and contracts that you will need to sell your dental practice without the direct assistance of a broker. You will learn how to prepare your practice in order to yield the greatest value, assess equipment values, quantify goodwill, write an advertisement, find and qualify buyers, negotiate the sale, and work with the staff and new owners to facilitate a smooth transition.

The Author

“After 25-years of practicing dentistry, I decided to sell my practice and was disenchanted with the way brokers were handling practice sales. In 1998, I founded Southeast Transitions with the goal of helping all dentists – large and small – effectively navigate the dental practice sales market. After helping over 1,000 dentists transition their practice, I’ve learned that the brokerage industry has a one size fits all model and that is not the best option for all dentists. There are many dentists that need assistance and direction, but with that direction they can become well-equipped to sell their own practice. I’m so pleased to offer the Bye-Buy Dental Practice program to this group of dentists.”

- Bill Adams, DDS, FAGD
